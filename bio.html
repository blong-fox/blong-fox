Short Bio

I study how life responds to environmental change across modern and geologic timescales. My work bridges paleobiology, ecology, and evolution to understand how morphology, symbiosis, and nutrient cycling shape biodiversity and ecosystem resilience. I also build and steward open infrastructure so these patterns are transparent, reproducible, and equitable.
