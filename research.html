Research | Four Pillars

Signature pillars

Four through‑lines connect my lab's work across data, organisms, and rivers.

1) Equity & Belonging as Research Infrastructure (ADVANCE + GWIS)

Treat inclusive mentoring and leadership as infrastructure that boosts research productivity and retention—and measure the effect sizes. Mixed‑methods, longitudinal designs, and exportable playbooks.

2) FAIR, Reproducible & Equitable Biodiversity Data (MorphoBank + BIOFAIR + AI)

Make morphology & collections data AI‑curatable, interoperable with environmental datasets, and credit‑fair for contributors. Deliver pipelines, schemas, and persistent IDs.

3) Symbiosis & Evolution in Chemosymbiotic Bivalves (Lucinidae)

Connect host morphology, symbiont diversity, and environment—extant to fossil—and deposit reusable datasets to enable cross‑domain synthesis.

4) Applied Freshwater Restoration & Treatment (PFAS + Kootenai)

Co‑design field‑deployable treatment and reconnection strategies, quantify biogeochemical responses, and release open, reproducible monitoring data.

Flagship, cross‑pillar projects

  • The BIOFAIR River & Reef Project — linking morphology, genomics, climate, and hydrology.
  • Field‑Deployable PFAS Solutions Network — multi‑site EO→IX trials with standardized QA/QC.
  • Mentoring‑as‑Infrastructure — reproducible, exportable peer‑mentoring and leadership playbooks.
