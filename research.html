Design: Paired riverâ€“connectorâ€“wetland sampling across pre-connection and connection windows (hydrograph-defined), measuring water chemistry (N, P, C pools), chlorophyll-a, phytoplankton, and zooplankton.

PFAS EOâ€“IX Treatment Train â€” mobile Ti4O7 EO + IX polishing

Configuration: Mobile thin-film Ti4O7 electro-oxidation (EO) with post-EO ion-exchange polishing (IX) in modular, parallel trains.

Notes: EO destruction followed by IX removal across PFAS classes; post-EO filtration added to manage electrocoagulation particulates; chloride displacement observed during IX consistent with anion exchange.
