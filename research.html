BIOFAIR â€” Project Website

Overview: The Biodiversity Collections Network (BCoN), in collaboration with the American Institute of Biological Sciences (AIBS), convened a series of domain-focused virtual listening sessions and an interdisciplinary workshop to design a framework for a Building an Integrated, Open, Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable (BIOFAIR) Data Network.

Focus: Integrating biological, ecological, and environmental data for equitable reuse across domains.
Methods: Stakeholder engagement, metadata harmonization, and FAIRness assessments.
Outputs: Workshop report, working groups, and a roadmap toward an interoperable biodiversity data network.
